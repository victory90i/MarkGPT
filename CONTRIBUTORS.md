## Contribution Tiers

### 🥇 Platinum Contributors
- 20+ merged PRs
- Sustained contributions over time
- Major improvements or new modules

### 🥈 Gold Contributors
- 10-19 merged PRs
- Consistent and impactful contributions

### 🥉 Silver Contributors
- 5-9 merged PRs
- Solid, reliable contributions

### ⭐ Bronze Contributors
- 1-4 merged PRs
- Helpful contributions to the project

---

## How We Recognize Contributions

All contributors are recognized through:

1. **This CONTRIBUTORS.md file** with your name and area
2. **GitHub's contributor graph** (automatically updated)
3. **Commit history** (your name on your changes)
4. **Pull request acknowledgements** (thank you message)
5. **Community appreciation** (periodic recognition posts)
