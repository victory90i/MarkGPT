# Neural Networks - Deep Dive Questions

## Question 1: Backpropagation Algorithm
Explain the backpropagation algorithm from first principles. How are gradients computed? Why is backpropagation more efficient than numerical gradient checking?

## Question 2: Activation Functions
Compare ReLU, Sigmoid, and Tanh activation functions. Why is ReLU preferred in hidden layers? What's the gradient flow problem and how does ReLU address it?

## Question 3: Network Architecture Design
How do you decide the number of layers and units per layer? Is there a relationship between network capacity and data size? What causes vanishing/exploding gradients?

## Question 4: Learning Rate Scheduling
What's the role of learning rate? How do learning rate schedules (decay, warm-up) improve convergence? Can you use different rates for different layers?

## Question 5: Regularization in Neural Networks
Compare L1, L2 regularization, Dropout, and Early Stopping in neural networks. How do they prevent overfitting? Which combinations are most effective?

## Question 6: Batch Normalization
Explain batch normalization and why it's important. How does it affect internal covariate shift? Does batch size during training affect inference?

## Question 7: Optimization Algorithms
Compare SGD, Momentum, Adam, and RMSprop optimizers. When would you prefer one over another? What are adaptive learning rates?

## Question 8: Interpretability of Neural Networks
Are neural networks interpretable? Discuss visualization techniques (saliency maps, attention), feature attribution (LIME, SHAP), and challenges in understanding deep models.

## Question 9: Transfer Learning
What is transfer learning and why is it powerful? How do you fine-tune pre-trained models? When is transfer learning applicable?

## Question 10: Research Frontiers
Discuss recent advances in neural architectures: Vision Transformers, Graph Neural Networks, Meta-learning. How do they extend traditional feedforward networks?
