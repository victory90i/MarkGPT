# Ensemble Methods - Deep Dive Questions

## Question 1: Ensemble Principle
Why do ensembles outperform individual learners? What's the mathematical foundation? How is ensemble error decomposed into bias and variance?

## Question 2: Diversity Requirement
Why is diversity crucial for ensemble success? How do you measure diversity? Can you have too much diversity?

## Question 3: Voting Strategies
Compare hard voting, soft voting, and weighted voting. When is each appropriate? How do you determine optimal voting weights?

## Question 4: Stacking Deep Dive
Explain the stacking ensemble architecture. Why do level-0 and level-1 learners need to be different? How do you prevent overfitting in stacking?

## Question 5: Blending vs. Stacking
What's the difference between blending and stacking? When would you use blending? What are computational trade-offs?

## Question 6: Base Learner Selection
How do you select base learners for an ensemble? Is it better to use diverse algorithms or multiple instances of the same algorithm with different hyperparameters?

## Question 7: Cascade and Strata Ensembles
What are cascade and strata ensemble methods? How do they differ from voting and stacking? When would you use these approaches?

## Question 8: Ensemble Size and Computation
How does ensemble size affect performance and computational cost? Is there a diminishing return point? How do you choose ensemble size in practice?

## Question 9: Uncertainty and Confidence
How can ensembles estimate prediction uncertainty? What's the relationship between ensemble disagreement and prediction confidence?

## Question 10: Advanced Ensemble Techniques
Discuss multi-task learning, multi-view learning, and multi-model learning. How do these extend ensemble concepts? What are emerging ensemble paradigms?
