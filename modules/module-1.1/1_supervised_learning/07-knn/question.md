# K-Nearest Neighbors - Deep Dive Questions

## Question 1: Distance Metrics
Compare Euclidean, Manhattan, and Minkowski distances. When would you choose one over another? How do distance metrics affect boundary decision regions?

## Question 2: Curse of Dimensionality
Explain the curse of dimensionality in KNN. Why does KNN performance degrade with increasing feature count? What are potential solutions?

## Question 3: K Selection Strategy
How do you choose the value of K? What is the bias-variance trade-off with K? Can K be adaptive or should it be fixed?

## Question 4: Weighted vs. Uniform Voting
Explain distance-weighted vs. uniform voting. When is weighting beneficial? Can weighting address class imbalance?

## Question 5: Lazy vs. Eager Learning
How does KNN's lazy learning differ from eager learning algorithms? What are the implications for training time, prediction time, and memory usage?

## Question 6: Feature Scaling Necessity
Why is feature scaling crucial for KNN? What scaling methods are appropriate? How would you handle mixed data types?

## Question 7: Computational Complexity
What is the time and space complexity of KNN training and inference? How do spatial indexing techniques (KD-trees, ball trees) improve efficiency?

## Question 8: KNN vs. Other Algorithms
Compare KNN with decision trees, SVM, and linear models. In what scenarios would you prefer KNN? What are its advantages and disadvantages?

## Question 9: Anomaly Detection with KNN
How can KNN be used for outlier/anomaly detection? What's the relationship between anomaly detection and KNN distance-based approach?

## Question 10: Extending KNN
How do you apply KNN to regression? How do kernel methods extend KNN? Can you design KNN for categorical features and mixed data types?
