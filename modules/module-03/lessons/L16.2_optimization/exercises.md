# Lesson 8 Exercises: Generative Models

## Questions

1. What is a generative model and how does it differ from discriminative models?

2. Explain Variational Autoencoders (VAEs) and their components.

3. What is the encoder-decoder architecture in VAEs?

4. Explain the concept of latent space in generative models.

5. What are Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) and how do they work?

6. Explain the generator and discriminator in GANs.

7. What are some training challenges with GANs?

8. What is mode collapse and how can it be addressed?

9. Explain diffusion models and their training process.

10. How are generative models applied in practice (image generation, text, etc)?
