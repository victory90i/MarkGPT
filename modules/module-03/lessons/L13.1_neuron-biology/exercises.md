# Lesson 1 Exercises: Deep Learning Foundations

## Questions

1. What is deep learning and how does it differ from traditional machine learning?

2. Explain the structure of artificial neural networks (ANNs).

3. What is a perceptron and what are its limitations?

4. How does the backpropagation algorithm work?

5. What are activation functions and why are they important?

6. Explain the difference between ReLU, Sigmoid, and Tanh activations.

7. What is the vanishing gradient problem and how does it affect deep networks?

8. How do you initialize weights in neural networks?

9. What is the purpose of bias in neural network layers?

10. How do you choose the number of layers and neurons in a neural network?
