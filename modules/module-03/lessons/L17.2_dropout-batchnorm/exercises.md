# Lesson 10 Exercises: Experimental Design and Ablation Studies

## Questions

1. What is experimental design and why is it critical in machine learning?

2. How do you design a controlled experiment for comparing models?

3. What is an ablation study and how does it identify important components?

4. Explain hyperparameter tuning and different strategies (grid search, random search).

5. What is Bayesian optimization and what are its advantages?

6. How do you perform cross-validation for robust performance estimation?

7. What is the test set and why must it remain isolated?

8. Explain reproducibility and how to ensure consistent results.

9. What are considerations for statistical significance in ML experiments?

10. How do you document experiments effectively for reproducibility and collaboration?
