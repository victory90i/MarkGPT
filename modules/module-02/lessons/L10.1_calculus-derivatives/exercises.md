# Lesson 7 Exercises: Supervised Learning I - Regression

## Questions

1. What is regression and how is it different from classification?

2. Explain linear regression and the concept of least squares fitting.

3. What are the assumptions of linear regression and how do you verify them?

4. What is regularization and why is it important in regression?

5. Explain Ridge regression and how it differs from ordinary least squares.

6. What is Lasso regression and when would you use it over Ridge?

7. How do you evaluate regression models using metrics like MSE and R²?

8. What is polynomial regression and when should you use it?

9. How do you detect and handle overfitting in regression models?

10. What are the practical applications of regression in real-world scenarios?
