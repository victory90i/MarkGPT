# Lesson 8 Exercises: Supervised Learning II - Classification

## Questions

1. What is classification and what are the main types of classification problems?

2. Explain logistic regression and how it differs from linear regression.

3. What is the decision boundary in classification and how is it determined?

4. Explain the confusion matrix and the metrics derived from it (TPR, FPR, etc).

5. What is precision and recall and when do you optimize for each?

6. Explain the ROC curve and what the AUC metric represents.

7. What is the F1-score and why is it useful for imbalanced datasets?

8. How do decision trees work for classification and what are their advantages?

9. What is the difference between Gini impurity and information gain?

10. How do you prevent overfitting in tree-based classification models?
